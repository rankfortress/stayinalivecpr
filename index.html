We are an American Heart Association Approved Training site. Our instructors have many years of experience in the medical profession and have been teaching to the guidelines set forth by the AHA. We are also an accredited member of the Better Business Bureau.The BLS Instructor-led course teaches both single-rescuer and team basic life support skills for application in both pre-hospital and in-facility environments, with a focus on High-Quality CPR and team dynamics.This course teaches skills with the AHA’s research-proven practice-while-watching technique, which allows instructors to observe the students, provide feedback, and guide the students’ learning of skills.The BLS Instructor Essentials Course is designed for Individuals who work/and or has a background in the Clinical Setting and wish to instruct the BLS CPR program.
